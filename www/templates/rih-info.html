The Crop Over festival of Barbados

Candy Bars
Crop Over is one of the most popular and colourful festivals in the caribbean. It dates all the way back to the 18th century. It originated from a huge celebration to signify the culmination of the sugar cane harvest. The Crop Over seasons consists of a bunch of different events which showcase many cultural aspects of Barbados. It showcases the crafts, music, arts and cuisine.
